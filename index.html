Sayali Lokhande

I am currently pursuing a Master of Science (M.S) in Computer Science at the University of California, Davis. I hold a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) degree in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Dharwad, Karnataka.

My passion lies in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Software Engineering. I have explored these domains through various internships and projects throughout my undergraduate journey. As a Research Intern at Durham University, England, I worked on the classification of plant species in a natural habitat under the mentorship of Prof. Paolo Remagnino. During my internship at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay, I was engaged in research on source-free domain adaptation under the guidance of Prof. Biplab Banerjee.

In addition to my research pursuits, I have also honed my skills as a developer through an internship at CareerBolt, where I worked with cloud infrastructure and backend technologies. I have gained hands-on experience in a wide array of technologies, including REST API, Node.js, React.js, and AWS services.

Driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to excellence, I look forward to enhancing my skills and contributing to the advancements in the field of computer science through internships and projects.
